Takawiri

This is NOT Mombasa or Diani . This is the other side of Homa Bay . This is Takawiri island with a sandy beach to boot. The beach is pristine; water clear as the sky and the skies bluer than the sky itself. This island resort was a hit. No electricity just solar. It is perfect for a romantic getaway and also for a day trip from the busy Rusinga Island. Mode of transport is a water bus to the island then a boat ride to the resort. This is a hidden gem of Lake Victoria
