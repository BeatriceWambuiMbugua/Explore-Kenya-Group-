Ol Tatet Cottages

The cottages built on a private land were first initiated by Chema family and friends. After realizing the immense potential embedded in the location offered, Oloika, the first cottage, was subsequently constructed in 2009. After 2 years, Ol tatet cottage was established. Never in a million years, would anyone imagine experiencing such tranquility in an African bush, and connect with the true essense of rural Maasai land. Ol tatet cottages specializes in making lasting memories.
